Background

    I read Computer Science at the University of Athens (2007) before completing my M.Sc in High-Performance Computing at the University of Edinburgh (2008). I obtained my Ph.D in Computer Science from King's College London (2012).

    I was a faculty member at King's College London from 2013 to 2018. Following that, I spent seven wonderful years as a Senior Researcher at CWI from 2019 to 2026. In 2026, I joined The Cyprus Institute.

Research

    My research area is algorithms and data structures with an emphasis on processing big data. I am interested in applications when there is a very good theoretical reason.
